# TE-4: Sybil under double auction

*Thought experiment, part of the [PromiseGrid Wire Lab](../../specs/harness-spec-draft.md). This file is content-addressable; its hash is its pCID.*


## Status

stub
A single human spawns 50 identities to manipulate prices. They must each independently build trust before their tokens are accepted. How long does it take their cartel to start influencing prices, and at what cost? Outcome: a "sybil resistance score" for the chosen scoring function.
