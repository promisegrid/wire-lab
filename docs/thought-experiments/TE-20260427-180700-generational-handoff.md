# TE-8: Generational handoff

*Thought experiment, part of the [PromiseGrid Wire Lab](../../specs/harness-spec-draft.md). This file is content-addressable; its hash is its pCID.*


## Status

stub
A `community-elder` retires after 6 simulated months. Their successor takes over, with a "succession promise" signed by both. Do counterparties continue trading with the successor at the same rates? Or do they discount? Outcome: a wire-level handoff pattern that preserves continuity.
